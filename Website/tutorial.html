How to make a cup of Tea

On this web page I will be teaching you how to make a proper cup of TEA

Ingredients and Utensils:

  • Your favourite mug
  • Kettle
  • Teaspoon
  • Water
  • Teabag
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)
  • Biscuit (mandatory)

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Get out your favourite mug and place it on a suitable surface.

  2. Step 2

    Pour water into the kettle and turn it on to boil the water.

  3. Step 3

    Put teabag into your favourite mug.

  4. Step 4

    Once the kettle has finished boiling pour the water into the mug leaving roughly 2cm from the top of the mug.

  5. Step 5

    Stir the teabag and the water with the teaspoon, the longer you spend stirring it the stronger the tea will become.

  6. Step 6

    Once the tea has become your desired strength take the teabag out and put it in the bin

  7. Step 7

    (Optional) Pour milk and/or sugar into your tea and stir it in with the teaspoon

  8. Step 8

    Now you have finished and you can enjoy a nice cuppa with a biscuit
